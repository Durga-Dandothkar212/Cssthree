About Temple

It is one of the holiest pilgrimage in North India, Kedarnath is situated in Uttrakhand. It is located at an altitude of 3,584m above sea level, near the head of river Mandakini. A trip to Kedarnath is an exhilarating experience.

What We Believe

It is believed that visiting this temple washes away all sins and brings salvation or moksha from the cycle of birth and death.

How we live

Staying in Kedarnath near the temple can be a wonderful experience for those seeking to immerse themselves in the spiritual and natural beauty of the area. However, it's important to note that accommodationsnear the temple may be limited and in high demand, especially during peak pilgrimage seasons.

Kedarnath Trek

monday 10am,friday

Kedarnath

The Kedarnath Yatra is a pilgrimage trek located in the Himalayas, in the state of Uttarakhand, India. The trek takes you to the Kedarnath temple, one of the four sacred shrines in Hinduism, known as the Char Dham Yatra. The traditional trekking route starts from Gaurikund and is about 16 km long, passing through beautiful Himalayan landscapes and villages.

Kedarnath

Char Dhams are the famous four holy places of Hindu Pilgrimages, which are nestled among the lofty peaks of Himalayas in Uttarakhand. This hindu pilgrimage Char Dham circuit consists of four sites: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath.
